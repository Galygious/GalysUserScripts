üîç Leads APIs

Get Leads

POST
/admin/fn/leads/getLeads/
Retrieves a paginated list of leads with filtering options. Used for lead searching and listing functionality.

Parameters:

page (number) - Page number for pagination
filterData (object) - Filter criteria for leads

Update Lead Data

POST
/admin/data/af/leaddata/
Updates various lead data fields including address, brand, timezone, and other custom fields.

Parameters:

leadID (number) - Lead identifier
fieldData (object) - Field data to update

Assign Staff to Lead

POST
/admin/fn/leads/assignStaffToLead/
Assigns staff members to a specific lead.

Refresh Lead Staff Listing

POST
/admin/fn/leads/refreshLeadStaffListing/
Refreshes the staff listing for a lead after assignments.

Update Lead Status

POST
/admin/fn/leads/updateStatusOfLead/
Updates the status of a lead (e.g., converting, archiving).

Update Lead Priority

POST
/admin/data/updateLeadPriority/
Updates the priority level of a lead.

Remove Staff from Lead

POST
/admin/fn/leads/removeStaffFromLead/
Removes staff assignment from a lead.
